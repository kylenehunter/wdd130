Trip Options Summary

Forest Flow: This calm, luxurious location provides an environment of thick trees where people can enjoy their surroundings. Our trainers will make sure the rafts are kept at a safe distance from any wild animals spotted, including bear and moose. Warning: If anyone is caught violating the rules the supervisor imposes, the trip will be cancelled for that person, and they will immediately be taken back to camp.

Mount Cruise: This river trip combines scenic views with moments of riding waves and rapids. Mountain goats have been known to be spotted in this area. Children around 8 year old are allowed as long as they are secured inside the raft. As with all rafting events, the supervisor and trainer will review the rules to make sure everyone feels safe.

Water Jump: This rafting river is for those looking for a more daring trip with their friends and family. Here you will ride rapids in Weber Utah and jump literal waterfalls. Extra training will be required prior to actually jumping the waterfalls. Our trainers will make sure each member feels comfortable and ready before attempting to jump the waterfall as a group. This river is for ages 16 and up only, though parents should decide if they want their teenagers riding this.
