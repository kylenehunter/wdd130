Learn More About Us!

Whitewater Rafting helps you schedule rafting trips for you and your loved ones. Together, we can arrange for a one-day activity, or we can arrange for you to raft for several days. You may bring your children with you, however depending on their age and the location/river, they may have to stay behind at camp with a responsible adult. We have permanent locations throughout Utah, though we also reserve locations throughout the United States as needed.

We will provide the supplies and trainers, while you just bring your group. When you are scheduling with our company, it is a requirement to have at least one supervisor and two trainers on your trip to make sure your trip goes without serious hazards. However, our workers are trained in providing safety without interfering with your group bonding times. They will also provide fun lessons for basic swimming, training what to do if a person falls off the boat, and proper ways to ride a raft down the river.
